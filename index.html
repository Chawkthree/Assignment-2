My Developemt Cycle

Plan

I start my web projects by gathering all of the content that will be displayed on the site. The content is then logically divided up into sections that will be divided up into pages. A wireframe and mockups will be produced so you can get an idea of what the finished product will look like.

Produce

I now take all of the information and start to code it into webpages. Along the way I backup my progress through GitHub. I use Sass, Frameworks, and Emmet to speed up my development workflow. I can also create media if you don't have any of your own copy, photos, or videos.

Polish

The site will now be optimized for fast delivery on the internet. I will also ensure that your site ranks well in search engines by combing through the content and making it as search engine friendly as I can. The site will also be tested in a wide variety of viewing environments.
